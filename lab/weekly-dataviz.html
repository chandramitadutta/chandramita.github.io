Why This Practice

Data visualization is one of those skills that improves slowly and then quickly. For a long time I could make correct charts — axes labeled, data accurate, no misleading scales. What I couldn't reliably do was make charts that communicated something worth knowing, or that were genuinely interesting to look at.

The weekly practice is an attempt to compress that learning curve. One dataset, one hour (roughly), one chart. The constraint is the point.
