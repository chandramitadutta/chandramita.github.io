— a note on this space —

This is my studio. Not a portfolio in the traditional sense — more like a workspace you've been invited into.

You'll find finished pieces here, things I'm proud of. But also experiments that didn't quite land, projects I abandoned halfway, and ideas still becoming. I show them all because I believe the process is as interesting as the product — and that honest, incomplete work is more useful than a curated highlight reel.

The walls? Those are mine. Books, films, writings, corners of the internet I keep returning to. Not data science work — but part of how I think. And thinking is where all the work begins.

— Chandramita

Hi, I'm Chandramita. she/her

Engineer, data nerd, and perpetual learner. UC Berkeley alum (MS Data Science, 2024 🐻) — currently building efficient, scalable data models and extracting actionable insights for the logistics industry at Flexport.

Before logistics: product data analytics in fintech at Bill, data analytics in cybersecurity at Deloitte, and data & cloud at Microsoft. Along the way I spent two years as a Graduate Student Instructor at Berkeley, mentored through AnitaB.org, and led gender equity initiatives in tech.

I'm drawn to the intersection of art, society, and science — the idea that data is most powerful when it's connected to human context. I'm an avid reader and listener on data ethics and AI, and a devoted fan of long podcasts about the lives of brilliant people in wildly different fields (best enjoyed while doing dishes).
